Your Journey to Energy Independence

Follow these seven essential steps carefully to ensure a safe, efficient, and long-lasting solar system installation. Safety first!

  1. Step 1: Installing the Mount

    The foundation of your system. Install the solar panel mounting brackets (roof-ground or flush mounts) for stability. Orientation is critical: South is ideal in the Northern Hemisphere, and North is ideal in the Southern Hemisphere. Adjust the tilt angle to 18 to 36 degrees for maximum efficiency.

  2. Step 2: Put the Solar Panels in Place

    Secure the PV panels (monocrystalline or polycrystalline) onto the mounting structure using nuts and bolts. Ensure the entire construction is adequately secured to guarantee durability and longevity against weather elements.

  3. Step 3: Install the Electrical Wiring
